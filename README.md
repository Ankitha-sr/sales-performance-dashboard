Sales Performance Dashboard

Tools: Python · pandas · Matplotlib · Seaborn

Dataset: Sample Superstore Sales — Kaggle

Records: 9,977 orders · $2.30M total revenue · 12.5% profit margin


Project Overview

Used a superstore sales dataset (9,977 rows) to build a Python dashboard
tracking revenue by region, category, and time period. Automated monthly
summary calculations covering YoY growth, top 10 products, and underperforming
segments — replacing what previously required manual Excel pivot tables.


Key Findings


Total Revenue: $2.30M across 9,977 orders with a 12.5% overall profit margin
West region leads in revenue, followed closely by East — Central and South lag significantly behind
Technology is the most profitable category despite not generating the most orders
Tables are loss-making at -8.6% margin and Bookcases at -3.0% — strong candidates for pricing review or discontinuation
Phones is the top sub-category by revenue, followed by Chairs and Storage
2022 saw the strongest YoY growth at +7.2% — 2021 and 2023 saw slight revenue declines
Automated monthly summary exports YoY growth, profit margin, and order count — replacing manual pivot table work
